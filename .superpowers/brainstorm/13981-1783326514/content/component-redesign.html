Component Changes Summary

1

LiquidGlassCard

Add noise overlay (3% opacity), enhanced inner shadows (top/bottom highlights), larger corner radius (24px), brighter border (0.15 opacity). The "hero" component.

2

LiquidGlassButtonStyle

Add subtle noise texture, inner top highlight on hover, press state with reduced highlight. Prominent variant gets glass tint + colored shadow.

3

LiquidGlassSidebar

Full-height noise overlay, right-edge border highlight, bottom shadow gradient. The sidebar is the largest glass surface — noise texture matters most here.

4

LiquidGlassToggle

Add noise to track, enhance inner shadows on knob, make the "goo" effect more liquid with subtle displacement. Already the most complex component — light touch.

5

LiquidGlassContainer

Wrapper component — inherit noise/highlights from material type. Add noise overlay, update border to match new opacity. Used for floating elements.
