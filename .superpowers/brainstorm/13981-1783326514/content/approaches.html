My Recommendation

Approach C — Full Liquid Glass

Go with Approach C because:

  • Your codebase already has 80% of the infrastructure (GlassView, LiquidGlassCard, etc.)
  • The noise overlay is a lightweight SVG/CALayer addition, not a full Metal shader
  • The inner highlights are pure SwiftUI overlays — trivial to add
  • You get the full "liquid glass" feel without the complexity of custom Metal shaders
  • Falls back to Approach A on older Macs automatically
